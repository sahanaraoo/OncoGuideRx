About the GDSC Dataset

The data we utilize at OncoGuideRx is sourced from the Genomics of Drug Sensitivity in Cancer (GDSC) project, a collaborative effort between the Sanger Institute (UK) and Massachusetts General Hospital Cancer Center (USA). This dataset is a powerful tool in the field of cancer pharmacogenomics and is used to predict drug responses based on genomic variations.
